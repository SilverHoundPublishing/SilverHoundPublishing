About Silver Hound

Silver Hound Publishing is a small press with a sharp bite.

Founded by two writers who wanted more from the page, we exist to bring back grit, guts, and storytelling with a cinematic punch.
We don’t publish for trends. We publish for tension.

We champion bold, character-driven fiction — stories that live in shadows, spill secrets, and punch harder than they’re supposed to.

We’re not afraid of the weird ones. The stylish, twisted, talk-too-much-and-fight-too-hard ones.
If it makes you laugh, shiver, or question everything — we’re in.

Our Approach

We treat every book like a film in the making.

From the first line to the final punctuation mark, we look for rhythm, voice, and momentum.
We edit like directors. Publish like producers.
Every story we work on is pushed to its full, brutal potential — not just polished, but sharpened.

We don’t do conveyor belts.
We’re a workshop. A studio.
A place where vision matters more than tradition — and risk is rewarded.

Pacing matters. Dialogue matters. Voice is everything.
If it isn’t unforgettable, it isn’t ready.

We’re not here to play safe.
We’re here to make noise.
