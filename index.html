About Silver Hound

Silver Hound Publishing is a small press with a sharp bite and a taste for the untamed.

Founded by two writers hungry for more than just formula, we’re here to resurrect grit, guts, and stories that hit like a sucker punch.

We don’t chase trends. We chase tensioncharacter-driven fiction that prowls the shadows, spills its secrets, and dares you to look away.

We back the bold. The strange. The stylish. The ones who talk too much, fight too hard, and refuse to stay quiet.
If it makes us laugh, flinch, or spiral it belongs here.

Our Approach

We treat every book like a film in the making frame by frame, beat by beat.

From the opening line to the final full stop, we hunt for rhythm, momentum, and a voice that won’t quit. We edit like directors. Publish like producers. Stories don’t just get polished here they get sharpened to cut deep.

This isn’t a conveyor belt. It’s a studio. A forge. A place where vision beats tradition and risk is rewarded.

Pacing matters. Dialogue matters. Voice is everything. If it’s not unforgettable, it’s not done.

We’re not here to play it safe. We’re here to make noise.
